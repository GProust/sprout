- Use, modify and redistribute the **code** under the terms of the GPLv3.
- Refer to the project by name in truthful, descriptive ways — e.g. "based on

## What you may NOT do without permission

- Publish a fork or derivative **under the name "Sprout"** or a confusingly

- Use the Sprout icon or logo as the icon/logo of your own app or service.
- Publish an app to Google Play (or any store) that uses the Sprout name or
  branding in a way that could make users think it is the official app.

**If you distribute a modified version, please rename it and use a different
icon** so users can tell it apart from the official Sprout.

## Why this exists

Sprout is a private health tracker for parents and newborns. Keeping the name
and icon distinct protects users from copycat or malicious apps impersonating
the official, source-available app. Reporting impersonating apps to the store
relies on these reserved Marks.
